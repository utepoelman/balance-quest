How to Play - Fraction Balance Quest

üêπ The Story: Help Nugget balance nuts on number lines! Each nut has a fractional position (positive = right of center, negative = left of center).

üéØ Goal: Arrange your nuts so your number line balances perfectly at zero!

üìè Visual Clue: Each nut appears on the number line at its exact position.

‚öñÔ∏è On Your Turn:

  • Click the Treat Bag to draw a new nut

  • New nuts appear below your hand until you discard one
  • Then select ANY nut from your hand OR the new nut to discard (double-click it!)

üå∞ Perfect Balance: If all your nuts balance at zero after discarding, you win the round!

üèÜ Scoring: When you achieve perfect balance, you collect ALL unbalanced nuts from your opponent's scale! Nuts are counted in 1/12 units.
